Time management, section order, and tips to maximize your TCS NQT score

Time Management

  • Foundation: 75 min for 65 questions (~69 sec/question)
  • Adv. Quant: 25 min for ~17 questions (~88 sec/question)
  • Coding: 90 min for 2 problems (45 min each max)
  • Don't spend >2 min on any single MCQ

📋

Section Order Strategy

  • Start with your strongest Foundation section
  • Attempt Verbal first — quickest to answer
  • Numerical next — formula-based = fast solves
  • Reasoning last in Foundation — most time-consuming
  • Coding: Solve the easier problem FIRST fully

Scoring Strategy

  • No negative marking — ATTEMPT ALL questions
  • Ninja cutoff: ~55-60% in Foundation
  • Digital cutoff: ~65-70% + good coding
  • Prime cutoff: ~75%+ in both sections
  • Even partial coding solutions earn marks
🔥

Last 7 Days Plan

  • Day 1-2: Focus on weak topics from heatmap

Coding Section Tips
